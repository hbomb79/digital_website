PAYDAY: The Heist


What is PAYDAY: The Heist?

PAYDAY: The Heist is a first-person shooter developed by Overkill Software and published by Sony Online Entertainment (now Daybreak Gamebreak Company).


What's the game about?

The game is based around pulling off differtent types of heists, including bank heists, jewel vault heists and many more.


How can I make it better?

Overkill have added on DLC called the Wolfpack DLC. It adds three new guns, two new heists and an entire new skilltree and sentry guns to place down. on top of that you can choose what mask you wear ingame.
